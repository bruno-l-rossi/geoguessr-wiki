### Step 1 - Identifying Martinique

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/martinique/0_mq_summary_new.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/martinique/Martinique_in_France_2016.svg.png)](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Martinique_in_France_2016.svg/1920px-Martinique_in_France_2016.svg.png)

Martinique, officially the Department of Martinique, is an overseas department and region of France, in the Caribbean between Dominica and St Lucia. As such, for country streaks purposes, it is **counted as France**.

Coverage is very limited: there are just three Generation 3 trekkers. Instead of learning metas to identify the territory, it is more useful to just memorise what the trekkers look like.

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/martinique/Martinique_Habitation_Clement.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/zSrUUsk3JPYyyP8YA)

Habitation Clément is a **rum distillery** covered by a trekker. Due to it being pinpointable, some locations are on maps such as [ACW](https://www.geoguessr.com/maps/62a44b22040f04bd36e8a914). There are several landscapes in and around the distillery, including:

- A [warehouse](https://goo.gl/maps/zSrUUsk3JPYyyP8YA) with a truck and a forklift.
- A [view of the outside](https://goo.gl/maps/FW2mfgwes23dj1yd6) with hills in the background and machinery on display.
- A [rural dirt track](https://goo.gl/maps/3Wx7QjXUcCUVRuRU9) outside with red soil and palm trees.
- The [inside of the distillery](https://goo.gl/maps/mnoysxVXLY5VZBpN7) with the structure made of H-beam steel.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/martinique/Martinique_Coastal_Trekker.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/pApNFF5yFbPfWrry5)

The **coastal southern trekker** can be recognized by a few unique things:

- [In the north](https://goo.gl/maps/pApNFF5yFbPfWrry5), the trekker starts off walking on a slightly hilly path, with other people.
- [Several houses](https://goo.gl/maps/XxD6aTzoBNrF5qPF9) with orange roofs can be seen.
- [A flatter and more open path](https://goo.gl/maps/DGkgXWthbCLEnskA7) can be seen, with the ocean slightly visible through the trees.
- [The coverage ends in the south](https://goo.gl/maps/9DxD1KWqV83XHPzh7) with a car park and some stalls.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/martinique/Martinique_Jardin.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/kmvFzPJvGuvtoBtj6)

The Jardin de Balata trekker is the most **tropical** and **humid** feeling on Martinique. The trekker can be recognized by dark and damp feeling, the trees with [buttress roots](https://goo.gl/maps/kmvFzPJvGuvtoBtj6), [ferns](https://goo.gl/maps/qBrahrtWA29634dU6) and [bamboo](https://goo.gl/maps/NDEVBU9sPAfnhvF6A). [This man](https://goo.gl/maps/Z3eXTHiJwapSoPU17) wearing a black shirt and grey cap can sometimes be seen with the trekker.
