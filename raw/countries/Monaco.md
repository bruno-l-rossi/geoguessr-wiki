### Step 1 - Identifying Monaco

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/transparent-mc_summary_2026.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Monaco_location.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/monaco/Monaco_location.png)

Monaco is a European **microstate** on the French Riviera, a few kilometres west of the border with Italy.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Monaco_Rich.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/uigMhSwBe6SnCU4MA)

Monaco is a **rich** and **densely populated** city state, with a Mediterranean feel to it. The architecture is similar to cities in southern France and northern Italy, but more luxurious. You will see a lot of apartment buildings, neoclassical details, and pastel colours. There are essentially no rural areas.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Monaco_Licence_Plate.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/monaco/Monaco_Licence_Plate.png)

Monaco’s licence plates are **short** and lack the blue strip.

You will still see a lot of normal French plates around, however. These French vehicles often still have the old yellow back plates, due to how old the Generation 2 coverage is.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Monaco_Hilly.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/3JveALZ5vX8AUkGH8)

Monaco is a **hilly** country. The very few areas that are not built up show mediterranean vegetation.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Palm_trees.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/mUwP7PCGfzLuZaWs6)

Planted **palm trees** are common.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Tunnels.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/E3Y9ws3uw87UwoEe7)

There is a lot of coverage inside **tunnels**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Monaco_Mopeds.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/EhdAvrCRYS7czP3y7)

Rows of parked **mopeds** are a common sight around Monaco’s streets.

### Step 2 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/monaco/Harbour_trekker.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/v3st3FYK3yrp3wVc9)

In the harbour, there is an extensive **Generation 3 trekker on a boat**. The Google employee’s brown hair and sunglasses are visible. You can see Monaco’s skyline and the hills beyond it. There are a lot of expensive [yachts](https://goo.gl/maps/4Z8rnvc99PPCqQEL7) around.

This trekker shows up a lot in the official competitive game modes, so it is worth remembering.
