### Step 1 - Identifying Isle of Man

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/0_summary_iom.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/isleofmanplace.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/isle-of-man/isleofmanplace.png)

Isle of Man is a British Crown dependency, situated between the United Kingdom and Ireland. So for country streaks purposes, it is counted as the **United Kingdom**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/gen2.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/TfswRwyEcPYGFiQu9)

Coverage on the Isle of Man is limited to **Generation 2**.

This camera can be recognized by the lower camera quality and circular blur above and below. Colours will also seem slightly off.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/IOM_Plate.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/isle-of-man/IOM_Plate.png)

Similar to the United Kingdom, the Isle of Man uses white front licence plates with yellow rears, but with a **red strip** on the left.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/landscape.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/JpxSJr5hjtau6qmo9)

The general landscape on the Isle of Man consists of **hilly farmland**, and narrow roads passing through them.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/architecture.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/pXdRg8A1rrDUuEqN9)

Houses with **whitewashed walls** and **entrance walls** are a common sight, similar to Ireland.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/kerb.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/eytGoWzJe1KHZokz8)

**Striped kerbs** can sometimes be seen along main roads.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/bollard.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/RkdWCyQMQuiwWdef7)

These **blue bollards** can commonly be found in urban areas.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/image.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/B6LMjcaXpaVc8xX57)

[Gorse plants](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ulex) (or Ulex), which can be recognized by their bright **yellow flowers**, are very common on the Isle of Man.

NOTE: Gorse plants can also be found in other rugged areas of the UK and Ireland, such as the Scottish Highlands.

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/0.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/isle-of-man/0.png)

Isle of Man is generally **quite hilly**, with a few exceptions.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/1.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/bc8aoiD167Ria5eb8)

Northern Isle of Man is **very flat** in comparison to the rest of the island. Usually, there are mountains visible to the south.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/2.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/EzfoFhoWrrDPDYBD8)

A **‘twig smudge’** can be seen on the left side of the camera lens in southern Isle of Man.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/Douglas_Metropolitan_Area.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/KQummfmpEc7z5sxa7)

Douglas, the capital and **largest town** on the Isle of Man, can be recognized by the rows of **terraced houses**, with a higher population density.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/3.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/LqRYiqeoQuEdshyL9)

Laxey is a town located in a **northwest-southeast valley**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/4.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/uhFraHPZzn5SHLXe8)

This generic lamp design with a **blue base** and a **small blue sticker** can be found on most of the island, except for around Douglas, and some parts northeast and south. In addition, the top 2 letters can be used to narrow your location down to 1 of the 21 [parishes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Local_government_in_the_Isle_of_Man#/media/File:IsleOfMan_LocAuths_and_Sheadings2020.svg).

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/5.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/NCL2XJcmVQkVzXJa8)

**All green lamps** are commonly found in Ramsey and Castletown, as well as more rarely in the Malew, Santon, and Garff parishes.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/6.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/FWdS4Ty4cWqNcSHF8)

A small **white sticker** **with 2 green numbers** will be present on lamps in Douglas.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/7.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/dBNU4sdoxZXvRCE98)

A **small yellow sticker** is present on lamps in southern Onchan. The 2 letters are the initials of the road name. E.g. ‘SD’ for Sunningdale Drive.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/8.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/6qqiBe33tvD52zd4A)

Many street signs in Ramsey feature the **oval shaped coat of arms**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/9.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Ei6i9JvHcSm7hqL58)

The **coat of arms** is placed **above the text** on many signs in Port Erin.

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/Airport.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/V5aB9X3qCWWpNHK5A)

The **Ronaldsway Airport** can be recognized by the large, flat and open field with a boundary fence.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/isle-of-man/10.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xenbgiQaVpEtGUYQ6)

**The Peel castle**, sitting atop an island, can unsurprisingly, be seen in Peel.
