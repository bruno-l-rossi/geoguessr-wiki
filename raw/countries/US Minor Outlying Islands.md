### Step 1 - Identifying the islands

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_countrystreak.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_countrystreak.png)

The US Minor Outlying Islands are a chain of islands that are US overseas territories. As such, it is counted as **the US** for country streak purposes.

The guide will be somewhat skewed towards the Midway Atoll, as that is the only island with pinpointable coverage.

NOTE: The easiest way to locate the Midway Atoll on the map is to zoom in slightly to the west of the North Pacific Ocean map label.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_gen2.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/PJEEw73taHwJb65u7)

The coverage on the Midway Atoll was made with a **Generation 2 camera**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_albatrosses.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Kakx6BbTK2Mp61HV6)

One of the most distinct features of the Midway atoll is the presence of multiple **albatrosses** on almost every single location.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_runways.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/hpfryhPkGfAgZqjn6)

A large part of the coverage on the Midway Atoll was taken on two **runways**, which are the only areas where it can be difficult to spot the albatrosses.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_otherislands.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/YALv3V46aVBbjXds8)

The islands other than the Midway Atoll can generally be characterised by three things. Firstly, the **Generation 3 trekker** coverage. Secondly, the low, **bushy** vegetation, and thirdly, the presence of numerous **birds**.

### Step 2 - Region-specific clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_nicerunway.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/F9kQ53oULpwe9pna7)

A **nicely paved runway** can be seen going west to east on the southern side of the Midway Atoll.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_roughrunway.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/9LnkL81nBAfS9Qym8)

A **poorly paved runway** can be seen going north to south on the Midway Atoll. The pavement gets increasingly poor to the [north](https://goo.gl/maps/d45BgC8WQFuxeAE67).

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_town.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/hokkxYeomeYi5MPq7)

On the eastern side of the Midway Atoll, you can find a **small village**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_southeastisland.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/1z9K9Q9iAftdDTTz6)

Southeast Island is arguably the least distinctive island in the chain. It has a small lake near the centre, and a large amount of birds in the sky.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_lisianskiisland.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/F5psr4siuK3fiKR9A)

Lisianski Island only has coverage right by the shore and can typically be recognised by the **large bushes** going around the entire island.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_laysanisland.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/cNLCGPvTvqv7LjXk8)

Laysan Island is the only island in the chain where the coverage has almost **no clouds**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_frenchairport.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/K6ZksuXBqhnuJbYs9)

The French Frigate Shoals is a fairly long and thin island with a shabby-looking runway. The island has by far the **largest number of birds** out of all islands in the chain.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_eastisland.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/oMVSWipvpK1mcT3U8)

East Island is the **smallest** island in the chain. There is barely any vegetation and you can see the ocean in all directions.

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_westcorner.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/i9mxerD8Z3eBzGQF7)

The western corner of the Midway Atoll runway is arguably the **most common location** on the official Geoguessr world map and may thus be worth remembering, particularly for a newer player.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/us-minor-outlying-islands/midway_tripods.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/gFcW5YXhgnCPArG6A)

Eastern Island, situated right next to the Midway Atoll, has two small areas of low-quality **tripod coverage**. It does not have any other official coverage.
