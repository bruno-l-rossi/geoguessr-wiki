### Step 1 - Identifying Pitcairn Islands

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/0_summary_pitcairn.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/Pitcairn_Islands_in_United_Kingdom.svg.png)](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/41/Pitcairn_Islands_in_United_Kingdom.svg/2560px-Pitcairn_Islands_in_United_Kingdom.svg.png)

The Pitcairn Islands are a group of four islands in the South Pacific Ocean and a territory of the United Kingdom. As such, it is counted as **the UK** for country streaks purposes.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_coverage.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/pitcairn-islands/pn_coverage.png)

Coverage is limited to two islands: Pitcairn Island and Henderson Island. The following clues will be focused on Pitcairn Island, as Henderson Island only has a very small amount of beach coverage.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_trekker.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/X4hZWbMktK2t2wGU6)

All coverage was taken with a Generation 3 trekker.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_landscape.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/uNGm97hA1hBZ6W47A)

Pitcairn Island is a semi-tropical and densely forested island, with most of the coverage being taken on small dirt tracks going through the forest.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_soil.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/fisP3ezsmwp3B9ZH6)

The soil is commonly very **red**.

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_adamstown.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/p17dnjEF3Q1FRo4S6)

Adamstown is the only settlement on Pitcairn Island, and is found near the northeastern shore. Some of the roads going through the town are made of concrete rather than dirt.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_henderson.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/DQwKDcto6R5jDH4D7)

The coverage on Henderson Island is limited to two **white sand beaches** on the northern and eastern shore.

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/pitcairn-islands/pn_toughslope.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/pNFtNFBajCSrPX8G7)

A dock with the POI “The Landing” containing a large number of small ATVs can be found at the eastern end of Adamstown.
