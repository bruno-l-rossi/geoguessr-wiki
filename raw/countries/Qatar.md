### Step 1 - Identifying Qatar

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/0_summary_qatar.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_License_Plate.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/qatar/Qatar_License_Plate.png)

Regular cars in Qatar have **long, white** licence plates with a **maroon** strip on the left. Trucks have **square, black** licence plates and police plates are blue.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_cars.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/hG3A7d8PmrJVREag9)

The Google car will usually be **blurred**, with only the **antenna on the left and** a sliver of the car visible. Sometimes the entire **white pickup truck** can be visible. Rusty clips can be seen on the crossbar.

*See the region guessing section for more information.*

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_landscape.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/93i1pngQ5JNxg9QP6)

Qatar's landscape is **predominantly desert**, with **arid**, and **mostly flat** expanses of sand as far as the eye can see. You will often not see a lot of trees or other greenery.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_Roadlines.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Renv5A1k3wqJDjmH6)

Qatar typically uses **solid outer white lines** with either white or yellow in the middle. More rarely, you can also find outer yellow lines too.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatarpoles.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/7H2syKvgiFZGhyvP9)

Qatar uses **round** poles with 3 insulators on the pole top, with the middle one being slightly higher. The crossbar also has 2 diagonal bars connecting it to the pole.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_chevron.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/vyBWdLGRZ45hig5T8)

Qatar uses **white on red** and **white on blue** chevrons.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/image.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/DWDixma3HcEcerM69)

Since some coverage was taken before the **2022 World Cup** took place, many banners can be seen mentioning the event.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Crossing_Sign.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/mj8vmvtKuS87w3tQ6)

Qatari crossing signs feature a man in a [**thobe**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thawb).

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_Coverage_Map_.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/qatar/Qatar_Coverage_Map_.png)

Qatar is pretty well covered, with almost every main road and some side roads having coverage. Only the south and west lack coverage at the moment.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/zones-of-qatar-2020-PSA.JPG)](https://d12eu00glpdtk2.cloudfront.net/public/images/local/zones-of-qatar-2020-PSA.JPG)

Qatar is divided into municipalities, further divided into **numbered zones**, which can be seen on [street signs](https://goo.gl/maps/M8fdWeriuhXw3Meu5). This infographic shows which number corresponds to each zone.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Short_Antenna_hint__281_29.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/azFQWo4CQjbTLxRV7)

This very **short** antenna is found in northern Qatar.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Middle_Antenna_hint_2.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/9z65ZJ3LMVrf5CHr6)

This **fairly long** antenna is found in and around Doha.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Long_Antenna_hint.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/mnrxxVzp4bsrCX1YA)

And this **long** antenna is mostly found in southwestern Qatar.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Qatar_Bollards_Hint.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Pyuj9bGnbTgSvySr6)

**Bollards** in Qatar are only found on a few roads, and you can easily tell them apart.

- The horizontal [road just west of Qatar map label](https://maps.app.goo.gl/gTG4kNoWi6Nvgzgd9#extra%5BloadMode%5D=latLng) uses black and yellow centre lines.
- The horizontal [road south of Madinat al Ka\`ban](https://maps.app.goo.gl/8zw6N5R1F83nVcoE7#extra%5BloadMode%5D=latLng) has a lot more greenery and, the coverage is also very overcast.
- The [coastal road south of Fuwayrit](https://maps.app.goo.gl/TccRhQzayTorZkmP9#extra%5BloadMode%5D=latLng) has coastal shrubs all around, and you can often see the coast.
- If the road doesn’t fit the above descriptions, guess on [the long vertical road west of Qatar map label](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Zc4PecYmyuGQXYju7#extra%5BloadMode%5D=latLng).

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/North_Stadium.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/zwUH6gceMPAxHJNm9)

Al Bayt Stadium can be recognized by the black and white **horizontal stripes** on the stadium along with the tent-like roof.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Lusail_Stadium.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/6YH83ZPGt8x8GdL9A)

Lusail Stadium, in Lusail, can be recognized by its golden **bowl-like** shape.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Lusail_Plaza.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/7GUmTgXzV8XGd5V99)

**Four towers**, resembling a **screw thread**, can be seen from almost every point in Lusail.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Far_Stadium.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/HkKKTNJvqY1EzSHr5)

A structure resembling a **gahfiya** can be seen around the Ahmed bin Ali Stadium, in Rawdat Al Jahhaniya district.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Far_Mall.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/9dcYpeDgJzE4ZCoL8)

The **Mall of Qatar** in Rawdat Al Jahhaniya can be recognized by the boxy appearance with many ads on the side of the building. Large [car parks](https://goo.gl/maps/HH42iSf57P7GxfVD9) are also seen next to the mall.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Education_City_Stadium.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/WQ7oQ2H33pDof6hm7)

Education City Stadium can be recognized by the large, **grey** cladding with a **diamond pattern**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Education_City_Pavement.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/YhvRmjh8VfszKDmk7)

Education City features distinct **black-tiled** roads.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Education_City_Signs.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/bTP8W4bfzVUb7JYi7)

**Brown** street signs, often fixed to the ground, are seen in Education City.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/South_Stadium.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/9nyFHpov4NtATegYA)

Khalifa International Stadium can be recognized by the **dual arches** shaped like a wide open mouth.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/South_Tower.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/gim4wCkSgGorF66m7)

The Torch Tower can be seen from almost every point in the Baaya District, and can be recognized by its **hyperboloid** structure, along with it being the tallest building in Qatar.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Cardboard_Box.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/382mjVZWvUYiSiMF7)

This **cardboard box** in the right side of the bed is seen in Lusail. The rusty clips will also be visible.

NOTE: The box will not always be visible. Make sure to look out for other clues before guessing.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Box_Left.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Mbi5imw2ScVxBFCt6)

This **black box** on the **left** side of the bed is seen southeast of Ar-Rayyan, around the green area.

NOTE: The box will not always be visible. Make sure to look out for other clues before guessing.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Box_Right.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/9c7MHTRHWV1cJkkv6)

The **black box** on the **right** side of the bed is seen in Al Khor, and in the green area around The Torch Tower and Khalifa International Stadium.

NOTE: The box will not always be visible. Make sure to look out for other clues before guessing.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/qatar/Airport.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Tn2mHDzDJipNFDfD9)

A **tripod** was used to capture the interior of the Hamad International Airport. A unique tripod that captured the [inside of a plane](https://goo.gl/maps/Bgbsuqhkc4g98q1E7) can also be seen here.
