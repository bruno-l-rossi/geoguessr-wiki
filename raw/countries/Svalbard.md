### Step 1 - Identifying Svalbard

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/0_summary_svalbard.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Svalbard_in_Europe__28-mini_map_-rivers_29.svg.png)](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/16/Svalbard_in_Europe_%28-mini_map_-rivers%29.svg/1947px-Svalbard_in_Europe_%28-mini_map_-rivers%29.svg.png)

Svalbard is an archipelago in the Arctic Ocean, of which Norway has sovereignty over. As such, Svalbard is counted as **Norway** for country streaks purposes.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/svalbard_licenceplate.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/svalbard/svalbard_licenceplate.png)

Licence plates in Svalbard are generally **black** with **yellow text**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/image.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/ZNCur2AKSqRa6fX79)

The general landscape consists of **barren, treeless** mountains. Trees and greenery are a very rare sight, with the majority of vegetation consisting of moss and grasses. The sky is also often overcast, with a gloomy look to the coverage.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/svalbard_russian.png)](https://www.google.co.uk/maps/@78.0652745,14.209516,3a,20.3y,279.31h,93.68t/data=!3m7!1e1!3m5!1sv7UE4xPKZmEqDVm4ZtNTjw!2e0!6shttps:%2F%2Fstreetviewpixels-pa.googleapis.com%2Fv1%2Fthumbnail%3Fpanoid%3Dv7UE4xPKZmEqDVm4ZtNTjw%26cb_client%3Dmaps_sv.tactile.gps%26w%3D203%26h%3D100%26yaw%3D190.49907%26pitch%3D0%26thumbfov%3D100!7i13312!8i6656?entry=ttu)

**Russian** can sometimes be found on signs.

### Step 2 - Town specific clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Longyearbyen.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/7w1UJdYJ3d9L5Cgr8)

Longyearbyen is the largest settlement on Svalbard, with many **boxy, minimalistic** buildings around the town.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Pyramiden.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/sSvva4XqadVvQpYH8)

Pyramiden feels more abandoned compared to Longyearbyen. Multi-storey **soviet-style** apartment blocks can be seen, however, with a lack of human-activity. As the name suggests, many [pyramidal](https://goo.gl/maps/BwYf7hXdrqow9e3S6) mountains can be seen in the distance.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Barentsburg.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/kEVChpQ91ctSeeu46)

Of the three towns, Barentsburg feels the most desolate. Distinct, **multi-coloured** apartment blocks can be seen near the top of the hill. Coverage is also more overcast and gloomy than Longyearbyen or Pyramiden.

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Longyearbyen_Road.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/SEo84AgAu4EjP62a7)

This road going south-east of Longyearbyen can be recognized by the barren, treeless **mountains** on both sides of the road, with **lakes** leading up to the mountains. A small black mirror can sometimes be seen on the left.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Airport_Road.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/UvnMUBrC7f39A1nK8)

A trekker can be seen holding on to a **red car** heading up a winding road, south of the Svalbard Airport. If the car is not present, this distinct [line of mountains](https://goo.gl/maps/Aub5RxFwVD9QruVz9) to the northeast is a good indicator that you are there.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/National_Park_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/fQbd5vYDLottV77H6)

This **large ship** can be seen around the Northwest Spitsbergen National Park. The coverage gets increasingly late toward the east, where it eventually becomes [dark](https://goo.gl/maps/5mYS6pc2bpQLMzS86).

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Wooden_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/ZpJw9Vg35N1SMnrGA)

This boat with a **wooden deck** can be seen in these places.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Small_Red_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/5WtkE4KeG5oMdAri7)

This **small red boat** with men in **red jackets** on board can be seen in the northwest.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Isfjorden_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/1vWX9vQ7i6g1Fknt7)

This boat with a **black deck** can be seen in Isfjorden, between Barentsburg and Grumant.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/svalbard/Pyramiden_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/H9NyBnHKgLSPoQQDA)

This boat can be seen east of Pyramiden, and can be identified by the **slanted front of the wheelhouse**.
