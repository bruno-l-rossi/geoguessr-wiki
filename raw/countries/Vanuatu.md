### Step 1 - Identifying Vanuatu

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/0_summary_vanuatu.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/vanuatu_locator.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/vanuatu/vanuatu_locator.png)

Vanuatu is an island nation located in the Pacific Ocean, northeast of Brisbane between the Solomon Islands, New Caledonia and Fiji.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/vu_trekker.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/MXbHWAVxi6XCVG6T9)

All coverage in Vanuatu was taken with a **Generation 3 trekker**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/vu_soil.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/iF2z775sCKFQC32C7)

The coverage was taken on a volcanic island, and as such you will often see **dark volcanic soil**.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/vu_tropical.png)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/n1F9nneDAhLeEjvy6)

Vanuatu is a tropical island chain, with a significant part of the coverage being taken in a **dense tropical forest**.

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/vu_coverage.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/vanuatu/vu_coverage.png)

Although Vanuatu consists of 13 primary islands, only **Ambrym Island** has coverage. Look for the ‘Port Vato’ label on the map, north of Port Vila, to find the island.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Volcano.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/TUdAm2qNf4gVrSXaA)

This unique **volcano** trekker can be found in the middle of the island.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Plateau.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/XtQPKEUtEh6N8ZEGA)

A distinct plateau with **dark brown soil** and **patchy grass** is seen east of the volcano.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Jungle.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/SwFibjUdmAbs2qoN7)

Tropical plants and many **ferns** can be seen in this jungle further to the east. A man with a **red cap** is also seen with the trekker.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Beach.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/V4XG6xGXdyLNzJd99)

A beach with **black sand** can be seen on the east side of the island.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Dirt_Road.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Tv2MdcJnNgLrmzAt6)

This dirt road with **tire tracks** and slightly purple soil can be seen east of the beach.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/vanuatu/Village.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/TGSzVWFb2dnVy1wn8)

Endu Pahakol, the only village with coverage on Ambrym Island, can be recognized by the small **thatched huts** and dark soil.
