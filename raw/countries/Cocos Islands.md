### Step 1 - Identifying Cocos (Keeling) Islands

![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/0_summary_cocos.png)

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Cocos_Keeling_Islands_in_Australia_-mini_map.png)](https://www.plonkit.net/images/cocos-islands/Cocos_Keeling_Islands_in_Australia_-mini_map.png)

The Cocos (Keeling) Islands consist of two coral atolls. It is an Australian territory, so for country streaks purposes, it is counted as Australia.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/image.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/h87frck6yQfSL11JA)

The general landscape consists of **bright white sand**, **many palms** and mowed grass. Cocos Islands is significantly less forested or hilly compared to Christmas Island.

### Step 2 - Regional clues

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/West_Car.png)](https://www.google.com/maps/@?api=1&fov=133.1644250522535&heading=58.31346630818494&map_action=pano&pano=t9toXJDM7shTLoa4pPytmg&pitch=-89&shorturl=1&viewpoint=-12.198487%2C96.838037#extra%5BloadMode%5D=latLng)

This unique car with a **snorkel** at the front right and the edges of the bed visible is found on West Island.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Bantam_Car.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/qZVyDP1CYvqWAcPi9)

This white car with the **rear edges visible** is found in Bantam.

NOTE: The car may be fully blurred. Make sure to look out for other clues before guessing.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Bantam_Pavement.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/BQmZ9KsvERmVkLkj6)

Bantam Village can be recognized by the **cross-hatched** brick pavement.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Runway.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/meTENoyBJQZjYXiB8)

The Cocos Islands runway can be recognized by it being the widest paved road on the island, with mowed grass on both sides of the runway and this [follow car](https://goo.gl/maps/evWNsAeXZ9ScSxqW9).

### Step 3 - Spotlight

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Trekkers.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/dHaidqjjuKGMWXTL9)

Cocos Islands has many trekkers along the **coast**. Most of them will be walking on white sand, with the ocean to one side and **palms** the other.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Direction_Island.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/kUodYKrF1H3Rb88fA)

Direction Island can be recognized by the beaches having significantly more **litter** compared to the other islands. [Bricks](https://goo.gl/maps/tSjoPrWjNQyRNRig7), [styrofoam](https://goo.gl/maps/Yvcoa9whDkWhEQDT7) and [dead coral](https://goo.gl/maps/3jQPVmeJT2PCunwY9) can be seen along some portions of the coast.

In addition, the trekker can be seen walking between many palms [inland](https://goo.gl/maps/63zUyWmqRJoKygM38).

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Horsburgh_Island.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/FafxVExg4rFmHUD78)

The inland trekker on Pulau Luar (Horsburgh Island) can be recognized by the **dry**, yellow grass, and more **sparse** vegetation.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Big_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/Dw2Vk1EmdQzicY1dA)

This medium boat with a **large seating capacity** can be seen from West Island to Bantam.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Black_Bar_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/m8SP5EdQ7BQJTU3E8)

This small boat with **black bars** at the rear can be seen in the north.

[![](https://www.plonkit.net/images/resize/1920/80/cocos-islands/Woman_Boat.png)](https://goo.gl/maps/f2PgYc65399fNNyP6)

This small dark grey boat with a **woman** sitting at the back can be seen in the south.
